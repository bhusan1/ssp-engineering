Who
Are We?

SSP Engineering & Consulting is a consulting firm, established in 2009, based in the Houston Metropolitan area. We provide expertise in Oil, Gas and Petrochemical facilities, Municipal and District Engineering, Construction Services, Commercial and Residential Engineering.

We have highly qualified staff and High Value Engineering (HVE) team with Professional Engineering (PE) licenses in 30 states and company registered in more than 18 states.

Our services include Structural and Mechanical engineering in oil and gas, Architectural, Civil and Structural Design, Project Management, Engineering Design, Surveying and Consulting services in municipal and commercial sector.

SSP handles onshore/ offshore projects with dedication towards customer's needs and thrives on result-oriented approach to finish off project with a smile on customer's face.
